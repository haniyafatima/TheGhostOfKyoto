Meet the Photographer

“I chase the light while staying in the shadows.”

Well, here goes. I’ve gone through a lot of loss in my life, and honestly, photos and memories are all I have left of some of the people I’ve lost. I really wish I had taken more pictures. That’s something that really haunts me til this day. Because of that, I pour a lot of my heart into the work I do. Every photo matters. Sometimes the memories are all that you have left, you know? Anyways, I’m also a huge romantic. Getting to photograph couples truly makes me happy. There’s something really special about watching people fall in love. I hope to be married one day too so it’s like I get to live out the dream myself, by capturing people. As for the name “TheGhostOfKyoto” — well, I’ve never really been one for the spotlight. I’m more of a behind-the-scenes kind of person. A ghost, if you will. Photography fits me perfectly because I get to chase the light while staying in the shadows. And, I’ve always been heavily influenced by Japanese culture since I’ve been a kid, so, yeah. There you go. That’s my mini story. Kinda poetic, kinda edgy, but that’s just me. Oh, by the way. My name is Cloud. I probably should’ve started this paragraph off by saying that, but that’s ok. If you made it this far, maybe you like me enough and won’t really mind. Thanks for reading.
